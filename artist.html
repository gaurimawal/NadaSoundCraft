RISHAB SHARMA

CONTEMPORARY SITARIST & COMPOSER

Rishab Sharma is one of the most promising young Sitarists of the new generation, blending the purity of Indian classical tradition with global musical sensibilities. A disciple of the legendary Pandit Ravi Shankar’s lineage, Rishab brings freshness and energy to the timeless art of Sitar playing.

Known for his innovative compositions and fusion performances across the world, Rishab’s music bridges the gap between tradition and modernity. His collaborations with Nada Soundcraft focus on creating performance-ready Sitars designed for stage clarity, tonal balance, and exceptional resonance.

He continues to perform internationally and conducts workshops to inspire young musicians to explore Indian classical instruments with a contemporary touch.
