ARTIST SPOTLIGHT: REVATHI MENON

MASTER SITARIST & COMPOSER

Revathi Menon is known for her seamless blend of immersive classical raga with contemporary improvisation. She is a dedicated follower of the Imdadkhani Gharana and has been trained by Sitar maestro Pt. Sharma. Revathi believes sound is the soul's expression, and her collaboration with Nada Soundcraft aims to connect musicians with instruments built with the same ethos.

Her Sitar is meticulously handcrafted with Tun wood, specially commissioned for its deep resonance and sustain. She currently teaches advanced Sitar techniques both online and through her academy in Mumbai.
